Which side should the filter drawer slide in from?

Consider: your movie pages have prev/next arrows on the left and right edges of the hero area

A

Right Side (Recommended)

Most common pattern for settings/filter panels. Keeps the main content visible on the left. Doesn't conflict with left-side navigation arrows.

B

Left Side

Less common for filters but used by some streaming apps. Would overlap with the previous-movie navigation arrow area.

C

Bottom Sheet

Mobile-first pattern (like iOS/Android). Slides up from the bottom. More natural on touch devices but less conventional on desktop.

Key Details

  • Edge tab — vertically centered on right edge, accent color (#b0654f), always visible when drawer closed
  • Drawer width — ~320px on desktop, full-width on mobile
  • Backdrop — semi-transparent overlay dims movie content when drawer is open
  • Collapsible sections — Ratings & Votes, Year & Language, Genres. Click header to expand/collapse
  • Close button — X in top-right corner, also closes on backdrop click or Escape key
  • Apply — fetches new movie, drawer stays open for iteration
